MORE ABOUT ERASMUS

With Erasmus, you can have the chance to study at prestigious universities in Europe. This experience will develop you both academically and culturally.

Located in the heart of Malatya, İnönü University blends the warmth of Anatolia with academic excellence. The experiences you gain here will shape you not just as a student, but as a global citizen.

Merging the wisdom of ancient Greece with modern education, the University of the Peloponnese offers a world worth exploring. Let the seaside campus inspire you, and take your academic journey across Europe.
