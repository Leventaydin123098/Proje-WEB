Minimum Requirements

Erasmus Application Conditions

Students wishing to study abroad with the Erasmus program must meet the following minimum requirements:

Need Explanation
Current Class At least 2nd grade
Successful Course Rate At least 70% of total courses
Grade Point Average At least 6.50 / 10.00
English Proficiency At least B2 level
